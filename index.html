FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my diet profile?

Select your preferred diet profile (Non-Vegetarian, Eggitarian, or Vegetarian) using the toggle buttons. Your weekly menu will automatically update to show only meals that match your diet profile!

What does Non-Vegetarian profile include?

Our Non-Vegetarian profile includes a mix of chicken, egg, and vegetarian meals throughout the week, with small portions of brown rice, oats, or sweet potatoes as carbs.

What does Eggitarian profile include?

Our Eggitarian profile includes egg-based and vegetarian meals, with small portions of brown rice, oats, or sweet potatoes as carbs. No chicken is included.

What does Vegetarian profile include?

Our Vegetarian profile includes only vegetarian meals, with small portions of brown rice, oats, or sweet potatoes as carbs. No chicken or eggs are included.

What time do you deliver?

We deliver twice daily: morning deliveries (7-9 AM) for breakfast/lunch plans and evening deliveries (5-7 PM) for dinner plans. You can select your preferred time slot when subscribing.

Are the meals really macro-accurate?

Yes! We weigh every ingredient raw before cooking. Each meal comes with a macro card showing exact calories, protein, carbs, and fats. No guesswork needed!

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Absolutely! There are no locks or contracts. You can pause your subscription for up to 7 days or cancel anytime through WhatsApp. No questions asked.

Is the chicken really from Anu's Chicken Shop?

Yes! We source fresh chicken breast cuts directly from our parent Anu's Chicken Shop every morning. This guarantees absolute freshness and quality that you won't find in regular meal prep services.
