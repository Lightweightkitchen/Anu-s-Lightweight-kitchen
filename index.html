FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do all meals really have 45–50g protein?

Yes. Every recipe is designed around 45–50g of protein from raw-weighed sources. The macro card with each delivery shows the exact breakdown. We don't estimate — we weigh.

What does the Chicken + Egg + Veg plan include?

A mix of chicken, egg, and vegetarian meals through the week, giving you the widest variety while still hitting your protein target every time.

What's in the Egg + Veg plan?

Egg-based and vegetarian meals only — no chicken. Protein comes from egg whites, whole eggs, and paneer, with legume-based meals for fibre and variety.

What about the Veg Only plan?

100% vegetarian. Protein comes from paneer, dal, rajma, chole, and moong — all weighed raw to hit the 45–50g protein target per meal.

What time do you deliver?

We offer three delivery slots: 7:30–9:30 AM, 12:30–2:00 PM, and 6:45–9:00 PM. You choose your preferred slot(s) when you subscribe — and on 2 or 3 bowl plans you can pick multiple slots.

Can I request veg-only meals on specific days?

Yes — just let us know which days you'd like a vegetarian meal when you subscribe, and we'll plan around it.

Can I pause or cancel?

Absolutely. No contracts, no locks. Pause for up to 7 days or cancel anytime through WhatsApp — no questions asked.
